Phew! April flew by in a blur of confetti, cake crumbs, and some truly unique mathematics. Yes, you guessed it – this past month was all about Nanu turning the big 7! And what an adventure it was.

This month, we only had one official *story* to share, but trust me, it was a blockbuster worthy of its own mini-series:

### 🎁 The Case of the Seven Gifts (and Then Some!)

Nanu's 7th birthday countdown was an epic journey of anticipation, carefully marked calendar days, and, let's just say, a *very* enthusiastic interpretation of how many gifts one might receive for turning seven. His theory that "seven gifts for seven years" was merely a *starting point* was both hilarious and, honestly, a little alarming for our bank account. Watching him meticulously count, recount, and then *re-recount* his haul (always adding a few extra for good measure) was a masterclass in childlike optimism and a subtle hint that maybe we need to work on those advanced arithmetic skills. 😉

### Dad's Two Cents

Seriously though, seeing the pure joy on his face, the way his eyes lit up with each wrapper torn, was priceless. It's moments like these – the slightly skewed logic, the boundless energy, the sheer delight in simple things – that make this whole "Nanu's World" project so much fun. Every day is a new discovery, a new challenge, and definitely a new opportunity for me to scratch my head and wonder "where did he get that idea?"

### What's Next for Nanu's World?

With April wrapping up, we're looking forward to May bringing some warmer weather, hopefully more outdoor adventures, and undoubtedly a whole new set of Nanu-isms to capture. Who knows what mysteries he'll uncover next, or what new mathematical principles he'll invent! Stay tuned for more Nanu chaos!
